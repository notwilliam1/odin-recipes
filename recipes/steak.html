Steak

This is a simple recipe for cooking a delicious steak.

Ingredients

  • 1 steak (ribeye, sirloin, or your choice)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (optional)
  • Fresh herbs like rosemary or thyme (optional)

Steps

  1. Take the steak out of the refrigerator and let it come to room temperature (about 30 minutes).
  2. Preheat your skillet or grill to high heat.
  3. Pat the steak dry with paper towels. Season both sides generously with salt and pepper.
  4. Add olive oil or butter to the skillet. If using garlic and herbs, add them now for extra flavor.
  5. Place the steak in the hot skillet. For a medium-rare steak, cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side. Adjust time for desired doneness.
  6. Use tongs to flip the steak only once. Avoid pressing down on the steak to keep it juicy.
  7. Once cooked to your liking, remove the steak from the skillet and let it rest for about 5 minutes before slicing. This helps retain the juices.
  8. Slice against the grain and serve immediately. Enjoy your delicious steak!
