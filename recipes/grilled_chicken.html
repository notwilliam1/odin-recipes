Grilled Chicken

This is a simple recipe for grilling a delicious breast of chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh lemon juice (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the chicken breasts with the olive oil mixture on both sides.
  4. Place the chicken breasts on the grill. Cook for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  5. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. This helps retain the juices.
  6. Optionally, squeeze fresh lemon juice over the chicken before serving for added flavor.
  7. Serve the grilled chicken with your favorite sides and enjoy!
